Checkers

Rules:

  1. Moves can only be made diagonally.
  2. When moving without capturing a piece, you may only move one square.
  3. When capturing, a player moves diagnoally over the captured piece. If you have the ability to capture, you must.
  4. You can make multiple jumps in a single turn, if the position of the piece allows.
  5. When a piece reaches the opposing side of the board, it is kinged and may move both forward and backward.
  6. The game is won when the opponent is unable to make a move. This can happen one of two ways: the entirety of a player's pieces were captured by the opponent, or a player's pieces are all blocked from moving.
